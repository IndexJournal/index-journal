CALL FOR PAPERS

Index Journal is calling for papers for its inaugural issue Identity Politics. Submissions are encouraged from art historians of all specialisations.

Manuscripts for review should be no more than 7,500 words, double-spaced, in accordance with The Chicago Manual of Style and accompanied by a title, abstract and brief biographical statement.

Submissions due Friday 31 May 2019
